About

In today's day and age, people are just one Google search away from deciding if they want to become your new client or not. localize will help ensure that your business has a complete and professionally built website with a custom domain name to put your small business on the map. However, a website won't be the only thing that people find on the internet - there are dozens of popular social media sites that customers use to write reviews, give ratings, and share with new potential customers. localize will manage as many as 10 different social media platforms like Yelp, Google Maps, Facebook, Grubhub, and more.

But wait! There's more!
As part of the service package, we'll also bring in an experienced photographer and photo editor for an indoor and outdoor photoshoot, so the next time new customers look you up, they think quality, professional, and trustworthy.
